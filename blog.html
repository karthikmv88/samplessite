iKall a smarter way to run your restaurant:

In this modern era where everything is fast paced and at finger tips customer service has become a vital key to drive business. Studies show that customers often visit a place not just for the quality of food but for the ambience as well. Keeping this in mind we at Indutech have designed a customer centric device called iKall which is a wireless waiter calling device.

Customer friendly experience

iKall, a wireless waiter calling device, is the first of its kind to be designed that works very well for a restaurant. With its very user friendly experience it certainly will win a lot of brownie points with the customers. It not only saves time but also can work as an analytical tool to calculate waiting time.

How does it work?

iKall has four buttons - one to call a waiter, another to ask for water, another button to ask for a bill and the last one to cancel an order. Every table will have these buttons. This works wonderfully as it not only cuts down the waiting time for a customer, but it becomes easy for waiters themselves to serve the customers better. At the backend one can even analysis the time from the pressing of the button to the service rendered. This is imperative to train the waiters better to work more in a time effective manner and enrich the overall customer experience.
